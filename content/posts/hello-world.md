Hey there! I'm Hanna — and welcome to my little corner of the internet. 🎉

I'm a 33-year-old IT engineer currently living my best life in **Valencia, Spain**, just a stone's throw from the Ciudad de las Artes y las Ciencias. Originally from Eastern Europe, I somehow ended up here following the sun, the food, and — let's be honest — a serious obsession with the Mediterranean lifestyle. No regrets whatsoever.

## The humans (and not-so-humans) in my life

I'm happily married to my husband Andrei, who tolerates my questionable music taste, my 11pm "quick code review" sessions, and my habit of dragging him to museums on weekends. In return, I make excellent coffee and I'm very fun at parties. Fair trade.

Then there are the real rulers of our apartment: **four cats**. Yes, four.

- **Miso** — the eldest, a grey tabby with the energy of a retired philosophy professor. Sits on my keyboard during calls.
- **Paprika** — orange, chaotic, and deeply convinced she is a dog. Fetches hair ties.
- **Pixel** — all black, suspiciously smart, has definitely figured out how doors work.
- **Churro** — the baby, named after the Spanish pastry, equally soft and impossible to resist.

They collectively own approximately 70% of our sofa and 100% of our hearts. 🐱

## What I do for work

I work in IT — backend development, mostly. Cloud infrastructure, APIs, the occasional 2am incident that definitely wasn't my deploy. I've been in tech for almost ten years now and I still genuinely love it, which I consider a personal victory.

Working remotely from Valencia is a dream setup. Fast internet, good coffee, a park outside the window, and cats sitting on my lap while I write code. Hard to top.

## What I do for fun

When I'm not in front of a screen (which, let's be real, is rare), you'll find me:

- **Running along the Turia park** — or at least starting with good intentions and ending with a coffee at a kiosk
- **Cooking Mediterranean food** with varying degrees of success — I make a genuinely good sofrito, and my paella is... improving
- **Reading sci-fi and fantasy** — current obsession is anything by Ursula K. Le Guin
- **Exploring Valencia** on bike, on foot, or by following whichever cat Pixel is staring at out the window
- **Occasional pottery classes** — I've made exactly one bowl that didn't collapse. I keep it on the shelf like a trophy.
- **Taking photos** of the city, the sky, the cats, and food I'm about to eat

## What this blog is about

A mix of things, honestly. Life in Spain — the beautiful, the bureaucratic, and the baffling. Tech thoughts when I have something worth saying. Travel, food, books. The occasional cat update (you've been warned).

No algorithm to chase, no niche to stay in. Just writing about what I find interesting, and hoping you do too.

Glad you're here. Stick around — there's more to come. ☀️
